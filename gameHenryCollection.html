It's all the Henry Stickmin games in one place! That's right! Breaking the Bank, Escaping the Prison, Stealing the Diamond, Infiltrating the Airship, and Fleeing the Complex, plus a new game that will be the Henry Stickmin finale.

Breaking the Bank has been completely redone. All the previous games have newly remastered art and sound. To top it all off, a new Henry Stickmin game was developed to complete the series, Completing the Mission! CtM is 3x bigger than Fleeing the Complex and features 16 Endings and 164 Fails!

All the Henry Stickmin games, including the new CtM, will be part of The Henry Stickmin Collection! Available August 7th 9am PDT on Steam! If you're interested in seeing some development logs and listening to the music from the HS games, please check out Puffballs' Newgrounds!
